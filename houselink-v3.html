Process

Four steps to
your perfect rental

1

Create your verified profile

Sign up as a Tenant, Landlord, Property Owner, or Agent. Verify your identity and unlock role-specific tools — all in under 3 minutes.

2

Browse or list properties

Tenants filter by location, price, rooms, and type. Landlords post listings with floor details, building sections, photos, and videos — AI-verified before going live.

3

Communicate privately & securely

Encrypted per-property chat threads. Private rent delay flags. No screenshots. No screen recording. Your conversations stay between you and your landlord — period.

4

Pay, receipt & maintain

Landlords issue instant digital receipts. Tenants track payment history. File maintenance requests with photos, follow repair progress, and rate completed work.
